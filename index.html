My Story

A graduate of Tongue River High School in Dayton, WY, I began pursuing a career in computers my junior year of high school. By the time I graduated in 2013 I completed 4 semesters of Cisco Networking Academy and various programming courses preparing me in languages such as HTML, CSS, and ActionScript. While keeping a 3.8 GPA in my academics I excelled in athletics, participated in clubs including NHS, Skills USA, FCA, StuCO, and served regularly as a part of my church.
Following high school I was accepted to Hannibal-LaGrange University to pursue a degree in Computer Information Systems and minor in Business Administration while also competing in Track & Field. During my time at HLGU my willingness to serve, be involved, and lead where shown through my role as Vice President of the Student Activities Committee and my position as a Resident Advisor. In order to further prepare myself for a career after school I worked many jobs on and off campus. These positions (details in resume) improved communicational, technological, and problem solving skills. After completing a CIS degree in 3 years, while keeping a 3.8 GPA, I will begin working at Ptolemy Data Systems as an IT Support Technician.
